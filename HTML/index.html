Introduction
This is a game called Catch The Fruit.
In this game , the fruit will dope from the sky. There is a basket on the ground. You need use the basket to catch the fruit. Catch the fruit as many as you can. If your HP == 0 or the time is over , the game is over.
Lose fruit or catch bombs will lose your HP.(fruit -5 , bomb -40) You can get some HP by catching fruits or recover HP tool.(fruit +1, HP +20) In Countdown mode , you can catch clock tool to recover countdown time.
Good Luck!

Control

Use the left and right arrow key to move the basket. Shift can speed up the basket. Space can pause the game. Esc can quit the game.

Mode

There are three modes in this game. Default mode is the normal mode. Special mode has more special tools and bomb. Countdown mode is same as Special, but it has a countdown time (3min).

Special Tools

Bomb can make you lose 40 HP or cut 10 sec. double can get double gain.(10sec.) clock can recover 15 sec. HP can recover 20HP. Ice can make the next 10 sec. slower. Magnet can get all the fruits and buff tool in the screen. Crazy will throw much fruit from the two sides.(10 sec.) Shield can defend a bomb. Speed can make your basket move faster.(10sec.) Easy can make your basket longer or make the fruit and tools smaller.(10sec.)

Theme

Four themes, default , real , weather , winter. You can change the theme in the setting page.
